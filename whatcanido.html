    04 shop items that help prevent plastic waste
    • Instead of buying toothpaste in a single-use plastic tube, swap for chewable tablets which are often sold in reusable, recyclable or biodegradable packaging.
    • Use a bamboo toothbrush instead of plastic toothbrush.
    • Use Beeswax wraps to cover and wrap food instead of plastic cling wraps.
    05 donate to environmental organizations

    06 adopt a turtle
    Sea turtles play an important role in the ocean ecosystem (Read more here). Sadly, sea turtles' lives are being threatened everyday as they mistake ocean plastic for food (Source). Why not adopt a turtle? Here's one organization where you can adopt baby turtles!
    07 go for beach clean-ups
    Going for beach clean-ups can are a great way to clean up our littered beaches, waterways and parks so that they can be trash-free! Check out more here (Plastic Soup Foundation)!
